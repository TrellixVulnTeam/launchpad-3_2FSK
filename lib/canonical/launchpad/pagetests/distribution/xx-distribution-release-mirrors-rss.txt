
To provide information about release mirrors to the ubuntu.com website (and
any other application/website who may want to list the existing mirrors) we
provide an RSS feed with information about all official release mirrors.

    >>> browser.open('http://launchpad.dev/ubuntu/+cdmirrors-rss')
    >>> browser.url
    'http://launchpad.dev/ubuntu/+cdmirrors-rss'
    >>> print browser.headers['content-type']
    text/xml;charset=utf-8

    >>> print browser.contents
    <?xml version="1.0" encoding="utf-8"?>
    <rss ...
