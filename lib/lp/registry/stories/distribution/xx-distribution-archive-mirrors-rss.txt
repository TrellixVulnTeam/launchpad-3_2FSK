
To provide information about archive mirrors to the ubuntu.com website (and
any other application/website who may want to list the existing mirrors) we
provide an RSS feed with information about all official archive mirrors.

    >>> browser.open('http://launchpad.dev/ubuntu/+archivemirrors-rss')
    >>> browser.url
    'http://launchpad.dev/ubuntu/+archivemirrors-rss'
    >>> print browser.headers['content-type']
    text/xml;charset=utf-8

    >>> print browser.contents
    <?xml version="1.0"...?>
    <rss xmlns:mirror="https://launchpad.net/" version="2.0">
      <channel>
        <title>Ubuntu Archive Mirrors Status</title>
        <link>http://launchpad.dev/ubuntu/+archivemirrors-rss</link>
        <description>Status of Ubuntu Archive Mirrors</description>
        ...
        <item>
          <title>Archive-mirror</title>
          <link>http://localhost:11375/valid-mirror/</link>
          <description>
          </description>
          <mirror:bandwidth>...</mirror:bandwidth>
          <mirror:location>
            <mirror:continent>Europe</mirror:continent>
            <mirror:country>France</mirror:country>
            <mirror:countrycode>FR</mirror:countrycode>
          </mirror:location>
          <guid>http://localhost:11375/valid-mirror/</guid>
        </item>
        ...
      </channel>
    </rss>
