= Team owned branches =

== Viewing team owned branches ==

Ensure that team owned branches are listed on a product's branch
overview page:

  >>> anon_browser.open(
  ...     'http://launchpad.dev/gnome-terminal/+branches')
  >>> anon_browser.getLink('klingon').url
  'http://code.launchpad.dev/~name12/gnome-terminal/klingon'
        
And on the product's detailed branch listing as well.

  >>> anon_browser.open(
  ...     'http://launchpad.dev/gnome-terminal/+branchlisting')
  >>> anon_browser.getLink('Launchpad support for Gnome Terminal').url
  'http://code.launchpad.dev/~launchpad/gnome-terminal/launchpad'


Ensure that a branch owned by a team can be viewed:

  >>> anon_browser.getLink('Launchpad support for Gnome Terminal').click()
