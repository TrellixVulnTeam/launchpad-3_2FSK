  The owner of a mirror can change all attributes of the mirror but the
  official_approved one. That's an attribute that can be changed only by the
  mirror-admins. When a mirror has official_approved == True and
  official_candidate == True, that mirror is listed as one of the official
  mirrors for that distribution.

  The +edit page is the one the mirror owner has access to change the mirror
  attributes, and that page won't let him change the official_approved
  attribute.

  >>> page_html = http(r"""
  ... GET /distros/ubuntu/+mirror/mymirror/+edit HTTP/1.1
  ... Authorization: Basic bm8tcHJpdkBjYW5vbmljYWwuY29tOnRlc3Q=
