== Launchpad front page ==

The front page includes buttons for the main applications

  >>> browser.open('http://launchpad.dev/')
  >>> browser.url
  'http://launchpad.dev/'

  >>> print browser.contents
  <!DOCTYPE...
  ...Join thousands of people...
  ...Publish your code...
  ...Is your project registered yet?...

  >>> people_link = browser.getLink(url='/people')
  >>> people_link.url
  'http://launchpad.dev/people/'

  >>> people_link.text
  'people[IMG]'

  >>> code_link = browser.getLink(url='code')
  >>> code_link.url
  'http://code.launchpad.dev/'

  >>> code_link.text
  'code[IMG]'

a search form

  >>> '<input type="submit" value="Search"' in browser.contents
  True

and lists of featured projects and marketing material.
