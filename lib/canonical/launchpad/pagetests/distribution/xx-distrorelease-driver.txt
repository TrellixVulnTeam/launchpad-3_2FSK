= Appointing a DistroSeries Driver =

Appoint Sample Person as the driver of the Ubuntu Hoary distro series.

  >>> browser = setupBrowser(auth='Basic jeff.waugh@ubuntulinux.com:jdub')
  >>> browser.open('http://launchpad.dev/ubuntu/hoary')
  >>> browser.getLink('Appoint driver').click()

  >>> browser.url
  'http://launchpad.dev/ubuntu/hoary/+driver'
  >>> browser.title
  'Appoint the driver for The Hoary Hedgehog Release'

  >>> browser.getControl('Driver').value = 'name12'
  >>> browser.getControl('Change').click()

After changing the driver, the user is redirected to the overview page where a 
message inform him of the driver change.

  >>> browser.url
  'http://launchpad.dev/ubuntu/hoary'

  >>> for tag in find_tags_by_class(browser.contents, 'informational'):
  ...     tag.renderContents()
  'Successfully changed the driver to test@canonical.com'

Sample Person is listed as the driver of Hoary.

  >>> for tag in find_tags_by_class(browser.contents, 'summary'):
  ...     'Sample Person' in str(tag.find(text='Drivers:').findNext('td'))
  True
