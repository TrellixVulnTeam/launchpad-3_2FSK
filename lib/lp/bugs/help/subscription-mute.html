What happens when I "mute" a bug?

You may have a subscription to a bug target (e.g. a project or distribution) that sends email notifications about bug activity. However, you may not be interested in a particular bug. In that case you can "mute" the bug and you will not receive notifications about it.

When "unmuting" a bug you are asked what kinds of messages you would like to receive about the bug.

If you are subscribed via a team, but the team delivers its bug messages via a mailing list or some other preferred email, the mute will not be honored because doing so would prevent the other team members from receiving the notifications.
