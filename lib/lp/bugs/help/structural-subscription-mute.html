What happens when I disable delivery?

Sometimes you will be subscribed to a bug target (like a project) via a team subscription, but you may not want to revieve the notifications. If so, you can disable the delivery of the messages to yourself without affecting the other team members.

However, if a team delivers its bug messages via a mailing list or some other preferred email, disabling email delivery will not be honored because doing so would prevent the other team members from recieving the notifications.
