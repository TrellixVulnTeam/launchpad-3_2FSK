Sample Person accesses the task page of a task on a private bug. He is
allowed to view the task page because he is explicitly subscribed to
the bug.

    >>> browser.addHeader("Authorization", "Basic test@canonical.com:test")
    >>> browser.open("http://launchpad.dev/products/firefox/+bug/11/+editstatus")
    >>> print browser.headers["Status"]
    200 Ok
