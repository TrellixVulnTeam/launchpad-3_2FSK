
It is possible for anybody to upload a BLOB to Launchpad which will be
stored for a short period of time, and deleted if unused. It can be used by
other parts of the system.

  >>> browser.open('http://launchpad.dev/+storeblob')
  >>> browser.url
  'http://launchpad.dev/+storeblob'

And we test the ability to upload a blob.

  >>> blob = 'abcdefg'
  >>> browser.getControl('BLOB').value = blob
  >>> browser.getControl('Continue').click()
  >>> message = 'Your ticket is'
  >>> message in browser.contents
  True
