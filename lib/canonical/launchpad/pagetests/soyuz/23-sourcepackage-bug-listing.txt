Visiting the +bugs page of a sourcepackage shows a listing of the open
bugs on that package, broken down by target distro release. The last
section of the report is "General Bugs", the section for bugs filed on
this package but not targeted to a specific release.

  >>> print http(r"""
  ... GET /distros/debian/woody/+source/mozilla-firefox/+bugs HTTP/1.1
  ... """)
  HTTP/1.1 200 Ok
  ...Bug listing...
  ...To be fixed in Woody...
  ...3...
  ...To be fixed in Sarge...
  ...3...
  ...Untargeted bugs...
  ...1...
  ...2...
  ...3...
