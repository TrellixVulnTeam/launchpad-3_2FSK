Making a "comment on change" when editing bugtasks requires that a
change was actually made.

    >>> browser.addHeader("Authorization", "Basic foo.bar@canonical.com:test")

    >>> browser.open("http://localhost:9000/firefox/+bug/1/+editstatus")
    >>> browser.getControl("Comment on this change (optional)").value = (
    ...     "I didn't make a change!")
    >>> browser.getControl("Save Changes").click()

    >>> print browser.contents
    <!DOCTYPE...
    ...There are ... problems with the information you entered...
    ...You provided a change comment without changing anything...

Let's try again, and actually change something.

    >>> browser.getControl("Status").value = ["Confirmed"]
    >>> browser.getControl("Comment on this change (optional)").value = (
    ...     "I *did* make a change!")
    >>> browser.getControl("Save Changes").click()

(Success redirects to the bug page.)

    >>> print browser.url
    http://localhost:9000/firefox/+bug/1
