
For each product/distribution we can see a list of top contributors.  These
are the people who got more karma by performing actions on these
products/distributions.

    >>> anon_browser.open(
    ...     'http://launchpad.dev/products/firefox/+topcontributors')
    >>> anon_browser.url
    'http://launchpad.dev/products/firefox/+topcontributors'

    >>> print anon_browser.contents
    <!DOCTYPE...
    ...These are the people who recently contributed the most to...
    ...Top Overall Contributors...
    ...Sample Person...
    ...Mark Shuttleworth...
    ...Foo Bar...
    ...Top Contributors by Category...
    ...Bug Management...
    ...Sample Person...
    ...Foo Bar...
    ...Specification Tracking...
    ...Mark Shuttleworth...

    >>> anon_browser.open(
    ...     'http://launchpad.dev/distros/ubuntu/+topcontributors')
    >>> anon_browser.url
    'http://launchpad.dev/distros/ubuntu/+topcontributors'

    >>> print anon_browser.contents
    <!DOCTYPE...
    ...Top Overall Contributors...
    ...Mark Shuttleworth...
    ...Foo Bar...
    ...Sample Person...
    ...Top Contributors by Category...
    ...Bug Management...
    ...Foo Bar...
    ...Sample Person...
    ...Specification Tracking...
    ...Mark Shuttleworth...


We also have a portlet which displays the top 5 overall contributors of a
KarmaContext (product/distribution).

    >>> anon_browser.open(
    ...     'http://launchpad.dev/distros/ubuntu/+portlet-top-contributors')
    >>> anon_browser.url
    'http://launchpad.dev/distros/ubuntu/+portlet-top-contributors'

    >>> print anon_browser.contents
    <div class="portlet">...
    ...Top 5 Contributors:...
    ...Mark Shuttleworth...
