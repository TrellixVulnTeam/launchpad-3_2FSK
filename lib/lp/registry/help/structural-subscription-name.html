Why do I need a "Subscription name"?

You may have more than one structural subscription per project, each with different criteria. You may provide a name in order to identify them in the future in order to edit or delete them.

Also the subscription name will be included in all email that is sent due to this subscription in a X-Launchpad-Subscription-Description header. You can filter your incoming email based on that header.

So, please provide a short, descriptive name to help you remember this subscription.
