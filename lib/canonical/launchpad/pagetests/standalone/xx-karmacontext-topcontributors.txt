
For products and distributions, We have a portlet which displays the top
5 overall contributors of that KarmaContext (product/distribution).  This
portlet has a link to the +topcontributors page, where we list the top overall
contributors and the top contributors by category (Bugs, Specs, Translation,
etc).

- First let's check the top contributors of the Ubuntu distribution.

    >>> anon_browser.open(
    ...     'http://launchpad.dev/distros/ubuntu/+portlet-top-contributors')
    >>> anon_browser.url
    'http://launchpad.dev/distros/ubuntu/+portlet-top-contributors'

    >>> print anon_browser.contents
    <div class="portlet">...
    ...Top 5 Contributors:...
    ...Mark Shuttleworth...
    ...Foo Bar...
    ...Sample Person...

    >>> anon_browser.getLink('More contributors...').click()
    >>> anon_browser.url
    'http://launchpad.dev/distros/ubuntu/+topcontributors'
    >>> print anon_browser.contents
    <!DOCTYPE...
    ...Top Overall Contributors...
    ...Mark Shuttleworth...
    ...Foo Bar...
    ...Sample Person...
    ...Top Contributors by Category...
    ...Bug Management...
    ...Foo Bar...
    ...Sample Person...
    ...Specification Tracking...
    ...Mark Shuttleworth...
