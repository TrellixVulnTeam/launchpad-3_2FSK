Trying to access a private upstream bug as an anonymous user results
in being redirecting to the login page.

  >>> print http(r"""
  ... GET /products/firefox/+bug/6 HTTP/1.1
  ... """)

  ...


  >>> print http(r"""
  ... GET /products/firefox/+bug/7 HTTP/1.1
  ... """)
  HTTP/1.1 303 See Other
  ...
  Location: http://localhost:9000/products/firefox/+bug/7/+login
  <BLANKLINE>
