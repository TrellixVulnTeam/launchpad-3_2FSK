= Removing an email address =

Any email address other than the preferred one can be removed from
a person's +editemails page.

    >>> browser = setupBrowser(auth='Basic test@canonical.com:test')
    >>> browser.open('http://launchpad.dev/~name12/+editemails')
    >>> browser.getControl('test@canonical.com').selected = True
    >>> browser.getControl('Remove', index=0).click()
    >>> print "\n".join(get_feedback_messages(browser.contents))
    There is 1 error.
    You can't remove test@canonical.com because it's your contact email
    address.

    >>> browser.getControl('testing@canonical.com').selected = True
    >>> browser.getControl('Remove', index=0).click()
    >>> print "\n".join(get_feedback_messages(browser.contents))
    The email address 'testing@canonical.com' has been removed.

    >>> browser.getControl('testing@canonical.com')
    Traceback (most recent call last):
    ...
    LookupError: label 'testing@canonical.com'
