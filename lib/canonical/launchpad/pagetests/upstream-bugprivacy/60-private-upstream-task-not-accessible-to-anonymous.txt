redirects the anonymous user to the login page.

  ... Accept-Language: en-ca,en-us;q=0.8,en;q=0.5,fr-ca;q=0.3
  ... """)
  HTTP/1.1 303 See Other
  ...
  Location: http://localhost:9000/malone/tasks/16/+edit/+login
