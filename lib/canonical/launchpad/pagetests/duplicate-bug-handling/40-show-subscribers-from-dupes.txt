Subscribers of duplicate bugs are shown in the subscribers portlet. When
there are either no duplicates of a bug, or no additional subscribers
added to the subscription list from the duplicate bugs there is no "From
duplicates" section in the subscription portlet:

  >>> from zope.testbrowser import Browser
  >>> browser = Browser()

  >>> browser.open("http://localhost:9000/products/firefox/+bug/4")

  >>> "From duplicates" not in browser.contents
  True

Let's mark bug #10 as a dupe of bug #4 to demonstrate.

  >>> browser.addHeader("Authorization", "Basic foo.bar@canonical.com:test")
  >>> browser.open("http://localhost:9000/distros/ubuntu/+source/linux-source-2.6.15/+bug/10/+duplicate")

  >>> browser.getControl("Duplicate Of").value = '4'
  >>> browser.getControl("Change").click()

The subscribers portlet for bug #4 now includes a "From duplicates:" section.

  >>> browser.open("http://localhost:9000/products/firefox/+bug/4")

  >>> "From duplicates" in browser.contents
  True
